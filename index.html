Daily Loaf Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Information We Collect

Daily Loaf collects your name, WhatsApp number, and delivery address when you place an order. This information is used solely to process and deliver your order.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to confirm orders, process payments, arrange delivery, and send order-related notifications via WhatsApp.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your information is stored securely and accessed only by Daily Loaf staff for order fulfilment purposes.

4. Data Storage

Order and customer data is stored in secured Google Sheets accessible only to Daily Loaf administrators.

5. WhatsApp Communication

By placing an order with Daily Loaf, you consent to receiving order confirmations, payment requests, delivery notifications, and reminders via WhatsApp.

6. Contact

For any privacy concerns contact us at: dailyloafdelivery@gmail.com
